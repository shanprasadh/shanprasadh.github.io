UC San Diego Library

Having fun isn't hard, if you've got a library card.

Having help content is not enough; you need to deliver it effectively.

One of the first and most intensive projects that came into collaboration with Fi@UCSD was the project we hosted with the UC San Diego Library.

Our team was simply tasked with researching how users were accessing help on the Library website. The result was a six-month long project to discover how we could put together solutions to address problems we found and changes users desired.

I had the great opportunity to form and spearhead this project to grow our student design agency.
